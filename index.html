Why Hire an Independent Contractor for Your Storm Damage Claims?

When your roof suffers damage due to a storm or other weather-related event, you’ll need to file an insurance claim to cover the cost of repairs or replacement. During these times, it’s ideal to hire an independent roofing contractor to help with the claims process. Here are a few reasons why:

Expertise: Independent roofing contractors have extensive experience in dealing with storm damage claims. They know what to look for, how to document the damage and how to negotiate with insurance adjusters to ensure that you receive fair compensation.

Unbiased assessment: Some unscrupulous insurance companies have their own contractor that may understate or flat-out reject your storm damage claims with their client’s interests in mind. An independent contractor, on the other hand, will provide an objective assessment of the damage and the cost of repairs. They don’t have any vested interest in the outcome of your insurance claim, so they can provide an unbiased opinion.

Protection: Your insurance company may try to cut corners and reduce their liability by offering a lower settlement amount than you deserve. An independent contractor can help protect you from this by providing a detailed estimate of the damage and the cost of repairs.

Quality of work: Your insurance company may try to steer you toward a preferred contractor who may not be the best fit for your specific needs. By hiring an independent contractor, you can ensure that you’re getting the best quality work for your money.
